About

I'm Kino Smead, I'm a Nonbinary computer scientist with a Bachelor's from the University of St Thomas in St Paul, MN. I'm drawn toward learning to understand complicated systems, both in my work and in my hobbies. In my free time, I solve Sudoku puzzles, play and theorycraft around the trading card game Magic the Gathering. I'm also an amateur media critic with an eye toward Japanese Anime and Western Superhero Comics, and the intersection of their broad mass market appeal with generally low editorial interference.

You should hire me because I have experience with Database Management Systems, as well as a strong understanding of HTML/CSS to create responsive and visually appealing websites. I also have spent a lot of time with "coding" survey responses into workable forms for databases or data science, and turning that coding into qualitative data analysis. I have experience with Python, SQL, and JavaScript, C++, Java, and am always eager to learn new programming languages and tools to solve problems effectively.
